This is the classic game of Rock Paper Scissor aka RPS, it is simple yet very complex. The game is played between two equal players (or in some cases a machine with superior intellect against a lesser.. I mean smart, super smart human). In each round the player must choose between Rock, Paper, or Scissor. After a dramatic pause, players reveal their choice. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. A point is awarded to the winner of each round. The first player to reach 3 points wins the game.
