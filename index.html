Hello, I'm Arnold!

I am the greatest Rock Paper Scissor player in the world, dare to challenge me?

This is the classic game of Rock Paper Scissor, simple yet very complex. The game is played between two players, in each round the player must choose between Rock, Paper, or Scissor. After a dramatic pause, players reveal their choice. Rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. A point is awarded to the winner of each round, the first player to reach 3 points wins the game.
